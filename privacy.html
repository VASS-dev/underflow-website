2. Definitions

"Personal Data" — any information relating directly or indirectly to an identified or identifiable individual (data subject) as defined in Art. 3 of Federal Law No. 152-FZ. Personal data is processed on the following legal grounds (Art. 6, Federal Law No. 152-FZ): consent of the data subject (provided during registration); performance of a contract to which the data subject is a party (the Public Offer Agreement); obligations of the Operator required by Russian legislation (tax and accounting requirements).

4. Categories of Data Collected

We collect and process the following categories of personal data: email address (for account creation, authentication, and service notifications); hashed password (for authentication); IP address and device identifier (for security and fraud prevention); service usage data (search queries, feature usage statistics — for service improvement); device information (OS version, app version — for compatibility and technical support). Payment data (card number, expiration date, CVV) is processed exclusively by the payment service CloudPayments and is not transmitted to the Operator.

Personal data is processed for the following purposes: providing access to the Service and fulfilling the agreement; processing payments and maintaining accounting records; sending service notifications (changes to terms, updates, technical information); improving service quality based on anonymized usage statistics; ensuring security and preventing fraud; complying with Russian legal requirements.
