3. Refund Policy

Refunds for subscription payments and purchased usage tokens are not provided. Given the immediate and fully automated nature of the service, the cost of rendered services equals the full subscription fee.

After cancellation: access to the paid plan is retained until the end of the current billing period; no further automatic charges are made; the User may remove their payment data from the Service. Cancellation of auto-renewal does not entitle the User to a refund for the current paid period.
