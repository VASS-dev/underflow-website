Questions

Everything you need to know before downloading.

Is Underflow free?

Underflow is free with limits. The free plan is enough to try the service and maintain a small collection. If you want to grow your collection seriously, upgrade your plan or purchase extra usage tokens. This model ensures fair usage and stable server performance for everyone.

Does Underflow sell music?

No. Underflow is a search and discovery tool. When you find a track you want, you click through to purchase it directly on Bandcamp, Juno Records, Beatport, Decks.de, or Discogs.

How does smart artist discovery work?

Our algorithm analyzes track metadata to identify remixers, collaborators, and featured artists. You get a visual network of related artists with color-coded relation types: R (remixer), F (featuring), C (collaborator), O (original).

What platforms are searched?

Bandcamp, Juno Records, Beatport, Decks.de, and Discogs. All five are searched simultaneously with unified results.

Where is my data stored?

Locally on your machine in a SQLite database. Your tags, wishlists, and collection never leave your computer. Search requests go through our server for speed and platform compatibility.

What operating systems are supported?

Windows 10/11 (x64), macOS with Intel processors, and macOS with Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3).
